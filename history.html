History of Chess

Chess originated in India around the 6th century AD as "chaturanga," spreading to Persia and Europe as "shatranj." It gained popularity among nobility in medieval Europe and became a symbol of intellect during the Renaissance. Formalized rules emerged in the 19th century, leading to professionalization and the establishment of international tournaments. Today, chess remains a beloved pastime and competitive sport worldwide.
